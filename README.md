# SenseBite  
**Making sense of every bite**

SenseBite is an **AI-native consumer health experience** designed to help people understand food ingredients at the exact moment decisions are made.

Instead of tables, scores, or raw nutrition data, SenseBite focuses on **reasoning, clarity, and context** — translating complex ingredient labels into calm, human explanations that actually make sense.

---

## 🧠 What SenseBite Does

Food labels are built for compliance, not comprehension. SenseBite acts as a **thinking co-pilot**, helping users understand what *actually matters* in a product — without filters, profiles, or setup friction.

SenseBite:
- Explains ingredients in **plain, human language**
- Highlights **trade-offs**, not moral judgments
- Communicates **uncertainty honestly**
- Reduces cognitive load at decision time

The goal is clarity — not optimization.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **AI-native by design**  
  The AI *is* the interface, not an add-on or chatbot wrapper.

- **Ingredient reasoning, not data dumping**  
  Only what matters is surfaced — naturally and proportionally.

- **Humanized explanations**  
  Calm, conversational responses instead of technical breakdowns.

- **Visual product exploration**  
  Clickable product categories (cereals, snacks, drinks, etc.) let users explore without typing or searching.

- **No scores, no labels, no judgments**  
  SenseBite doesn’t classify food as “good” or “bad” and does not provide medical advice.

---

## 🚫 What SenseBite Is Not

- ❌ A nutrition tracker  
- ❌ A database browser  
- ❌ A raw ingredient lookup tool  
- ❌ A health scoring or ranking app  
- ❌ An AI chatbot pasted onto a form  

SenseBite is designed around **understanding**, not information overload.

---

## 🧩 How It Works (Conceptually)

1. A user pastes an ingredient list or selects a product category  
2. The system infers what’s relevant — without asking setup questions  
3. The AI reasons internally  
4. A **single, human explanation** is produced  
5. The user leaves with clarity and confidence

The experience *is* the product.  
The data is just a supporting layer.

---

## 🗂️ Data & Grounding

SenseBite may optionally draw from:
- Public food datasets (e.g. Open Food Facts)
- Small, curated context sets for ingredient understanding

These sources are **never exposed directly to users**.  
Value comes from **reasoned explanation**, not data completeness.

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

SenseBite provides **informational clarity**, not medical advice.  
All explanations are educational and intended to support understanding — not diagnosis or treatment.
