You are extending an existing AI-native web app called SenseBite.

Your task is to add clickable product categories that allow users to explore common food products by clicking on an image, without typing or searching.

Do not turn this into a browsing app, catalog, or database.

Purpose of Categories
Categories are entry shortcuts, not features.

They exist only to:

Reduce friction

Let users start the AI experience instantly

Demonstrate SenseBite’s reasoning on familiar products

Category UI Behavior
Display categories as image-based cards

Each card should show:

A product image

A short category label

No brand names

No ingredient lists visible to the user

No filters, dropdowns, or search inside categories

Place categories near the main input with a soft label like:

“Or explore common products”

Required Categories (Use These)
Add 6–8 common categories such as:

Breakfast cereals

Packaged snacks

Soft drinks

Instant foods (noodles, soups)

Chocolates & sweets

Dairy products

Energy or sports drinks

Packaged bread & bakery

Images can be generic stock-style visuals.

Click Behavior (Very Important)
When a user clicks a category:

Load a predefined sample ingredient list (mock data is fine)

Automatically trigger the existing AI explanation flow

Do not show the ingredients directly

Do not explain where the data came from

The experience should feel like:

“I clicked → SenseBite immediately explains”

AI Response Rules (Must Stay Consistent)
Use the existing human, Version-C response style

No structured ingredient breakdowns

No boxes, lists, or sections

No reasoning layer mentions

Calm, natural, human explanation

Mention only what matters, naturally

Acknowledge processing without alarm

End with a soft sense of perspective (e.g. occasional vs everyday)

Data Rules
Ingredient data can be mocked or simulated

Accuracy and completeness are not important

Do not surface raw data or nutrition tables

Categories must not feel like a database

What NOT to Add
No product comparison

No “popular items”

No scrolling product lists

No scores, labels, or ratings

No search inside categories

Final Instruction
Categories should feel like friendly shortcuts into understanding, not a browsing feature.

The moment a category is clicked, the AI should take over and do the cognitive work for the user.

If someone removes the categories, the core app should still make sense.
